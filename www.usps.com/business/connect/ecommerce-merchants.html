                USPS Connect eCommerce

                For Platforms/Marketplaces | For Merchants/Sellers

                Merchants/Sellers looking to access USPS® products and services can expect the following benefits through USPS Connect® eCommerce:

                • Different access options
                • Discounted rates
                • No minimum volume

                Find a platform and contact them directly to get started! If you would like to learn more, contact a USPS representative.

                Learn More

                USPS Connect eCommerce Benefits

                For Merchants/Sellers

                • Get discounted rates through participating platforms
                • No minimum volume commitments
                • USPS Tracking® and insurance available for most shipments (restrictions apply)1
                • Free packaging available, includes flat rate and other options
                • Free Package Pickup service2
                • Free Saturday delivery3
                • No fuel surcharges
                • No residential delivery surcharges
                • No additional charge for address correction, forwarding, or returning undeliverable items for certain products
                • Convenient package returns solutions

                For Platforms/Marketplaces

                If youre an ecommerce platform who wants exclusive pricing discounts on USPS shipping (with no minimum shipping volumes) that you can pass along to merchants, learn more and apply.
