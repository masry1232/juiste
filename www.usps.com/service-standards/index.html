                USPS Service Standards Map

                The USPS® Service Standards Map allows you to view expected delivery times for domestic mailing & shipping services.

                Exceptions apply and delivery time is not guaranteed.

                Get Started!

                • Enter the 5-digit ZIP Code™ you're shipping from.
                • Drag and zoom the map to see the area you're mailing to, as well as Alaska, Hawaii, and U.S. territories that receive Priority Mail® service.
