Common Questions

How do I report a scam payment?

  1. Select the payment you believe was a scam.

  2. Tap the “...” in the top right corner.

  3. Select “Report an Issue.”

  4. Select “I was scammed.”

Note: you can report a payment you believe was a scam within 30 days

How do I report and block a scam account?

  1. Launch the Cash App and tap on a Customer Avatar to view a profile. You can also search for a profile by entering a Name, $Cashtag,  Phone Number, or Email

  2. Scroll to the bottom of the profile and tap on “Report” or “Block”

  3. Select one of the options and follow next steps

How can I make sure a Cash App giveaway is real and not a scam?

Copycat giveaways might look and feel like the real thing—but there are some easy ways to tell if the giveaway is for real.

You’ll know the giveaway is a scam if:

  • You’re asked to pay to participate. 

  • You’re asked to share personal or login information in social comments or DMs.

  • The spelling of the account name or url is incorrect.

How to make sure it’s a real Cash App giveaway:

  • Check the website url. We post giveaways on “cash.app.” url or reputable partner companies we collaborate with.

  • Check the spelling of the account name. Cash App will only post on its official verified channels on social media via @CashApp.

  • Review the details on the website. All Cash App giveaways will have respective rules with a Cash.App domain that will link back to a url that begins with “cash.app.”
