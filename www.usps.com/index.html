                Mail Your Tax Return

                If your tax return is properly addressed, has enough postage, and is postmarked by April 15, 2025, the IRS considers it on time. On or before the deadline, take your tax return to your local Post Office location, go to the retail counter, and ask the retail associate to postmark it.
