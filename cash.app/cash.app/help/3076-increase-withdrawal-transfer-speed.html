Increase Withdrawal Transfer Speed

Having a debit card linked to your Cash App may let you convert pending Standard transfers to Instant transfers:

  1. Tap the Activity tab on your Cash App home screen

  2. Choose the deposit to expedite

  3. Select Transfer Instantly

If the button is unavailable, then the transfer has already been sent and cannot be expedited. The transferred money should arrive within 1–3 business days.
